1.复数的欧几里得范数

Wiki链接： https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E8%8C%83%E6%95%B0

如果将复平面看作欧几里得平面$\mathbb{R}^2$，那么复数的欧几里得范数是其绝对值（又称为模）。这样，我们可把$x+iy$视为欧几里得平面上的一个向量，由此，这个向量的欧几里得范数即为$\sqrt{x^2+y^2}$（最初由欧拉提出）。

2.狄拉克符号

Wiki链接： https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E7%8B%84%E6%8B%89%E5%85%8B%E7%AC%A6%E5%8F%B7

  • bracket n.括号 -> bra-ket -> bra vector($\langle \psi|$)，ket vector($|\psi \rangle$)

  • The $|\psi \rangle$ notation is called Dirac notation, used to represent quantum states.

  • Mathematically, $|\psi \rangle$ (“ket vector”) is a column vector.

  • The dual/Hermitian conjugate of column vectors (i.e. row vectors), are called “bra vectors”:

                      
$|\psi \rangle = \left(\begin{array}{l} \alpha \ \beta \end{array}\right)$
                      
$\langle \psi|=(\alpha^*,\beta^*)=\alpha^\langle 0|+\beta^\langle 1|$

                           
“ket psi”
                          
“bra psi”
        
$\alpha^*,\beta^*$ are complex conjugates of $\alpha,\beta$.

  • duals of the standard basis vectors:$\langle 0| = (1,0)$ and $\langle 1| = (0,1)$.

  • bra vector is a row vector,ket vector is a column vector.(bra vector是行向量，ket vector是列向量。)

Example:

1.$|\psi \rangle = \left(\begin{array}{l} 1 \ 0 \end{array}\right)$,$\langle \psi| = (1,0)$

2.$|\psi \rangle = \left(\begin{array}{l} i \ \sqrt{3}-5i \end{array}\right)$,$\langle \psi| = (-i,\sqrt{3}+5i)$
